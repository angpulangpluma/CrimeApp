- WHAT IS SAFE SPACE? -



The Philippines is one of the most beautiful countries in the world where it even earned its title as the Pearl of the Orient but due to crime we often doubt the beauty of this nation because of a lack in safety and security. The application aims to help people not just make people be more secured but also make them feel safer in their own space. It is a cross platform application that helps people be more informed and aware about the criminal activity around their area in the Philippines.

FEATURES:

  • Criminal Activity Assessment of the Different Areas in the Philippines
  • Contains Data Regarding Recent Criminal Activities Reported In the Areas
  • Informs the user when they are nearing a high risk area

WHERE WILL THE DATA COME FROM?

The data that is used by the application are taken from various sources. One of the main sources of information would be government agencies willing to share their information publicly such as the Philippine National Police and the Philippine Statistics Authority. Another source of information of would be third party organizations who have credibility such as The Philippine Inquirer and CNN Philippines. Processing information is done with the use of Deep Learning to identify possible anomalies in the data gathered.
